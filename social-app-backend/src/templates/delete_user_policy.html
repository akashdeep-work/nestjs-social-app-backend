Delete User Policy

Effective date: September 1, 2025 • Last updated: September 4, 2025

1. Introduction

This Delete User Policy explains how users of Social App ("we", "our", "us") can request deletion of their account and associated personal data. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.

2. Requesting Deletion

You can request deletion of your account through the following methods:

  • In-app: Navigate to Settings → Account → Delete Account and follow the prompts.
  • Email: Contact our support team at happiibook@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete My Account.”

3. What Happens When You Delete

  • Your profile, posts, messages, and connections will be permanently deleted or anonymized, unless retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
  • Content you shared with others (e.g., messages, comments) may remain visible to them even after deletion.
  • Some backup copies may be retained for up to 1 year for disaster recovery, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

4. Exceptions

We may retain certain data even after account deletion when:

  • Required by applicable law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Necessary for resolving disputes, enforcing our agreements, or detecting/preventing fraud and abuse.
  • Aggregate or anonymized data that cannot reasonably identify you may be retained for analytics and reporting.

5. Reactivation

Deleted accounts cannot be reactivated. If you wish to use the Services again, you will need to create a new account.

6. Children’s Accounts

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 years has created an account without your consent, please contact us immediately at happiibook@gmail.com. We will delete the account and associated data promptly.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Delete User Policy or need assistance, contact us at happiibook@gmail.com or write to us at: Happiibook LLC dba Social App, 1810 E. Sahara Ave Suite 212, Las Vegas, Nevada 89104.
